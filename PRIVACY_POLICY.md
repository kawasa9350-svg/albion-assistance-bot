# Privacy Policy - Albion Assistance Bot

**Last Updated:** [Date]

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Albion Assistance Bot (the "Bot", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our Discord bot service. By using the Bot, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Discord Information
When you interact with the Bot, we automatically collect:
- **Discord User ID**: A unique identifier for your Discord account
- **Discord Username and Tag**: Your display name and discriminator
- **Guild (Server) ID**: Unique identifier for Discord servers where the Bot is used
- **Guild Name**: The name of Discord servers where the Bot is used
- **Channel IDs**: Identifiers for channels where Bot commands are used
- **Message IDs**: Identifiers for messages related to Bot interactions
- **Role IDs**: Identifiers for Discord roles with Bot permissions

### 2.2 User Registration Data
When you register with the Bot, we collect:
- **In-Game Name**: Your Albion Online character name
- **Registration Timestamp**: Date and time when you registered
- **Update Timestamps**: Dates when your information was last modified

### 2.3 Financial Data
For balance and payment features, we collect:
- **Balance Information**: Virtual currency balances associated with your account
- **Transaction History**: Records of payments, paychecks, and balance adjustments
- **Loot Split Data**: Information about loot distribution and splits

### 2.4 Activity Data
We collect data about your activity:
- **Attendance Records**: Tracking of your participation in events
- **Event Signups**: Your signups for scheduled events
- **Composition Signups**: Your signups for group compositions
- **Command Usage**: Records of commands you use (for functionality purposes)

### 2.5 Content Data
We store content you create:
- **Build Data**: Equipment builds including weapons, armor, food, potions, and other gear
- **Composition Data**: Group compositions and team setups you create
- **Event Data**: Events you create including dates, times, and content types
- **Content Types**: Categories of content you define (e.g., "Dungeon", "PvP")
- **Inventory Data**: Gear and items stored in guild inventory

### 2.6 Administrative Data
For server administration, we collect:
- **Permission Settings**: Role-based command permissions
- **Guild Prefix**: Custom command prefixes for servers
- **Tax Rates**: Tax rate configurations for different content types
- **Voice Channel Setup**: Configuration for voice channel features
- **Regear Reservations**: Records of gear reservations and distributions

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Service Provision
We use collected information to:
- Provide Bot functionality and features
- Process commands and interactions
- Maintain user registrations and profiles
- Track attendance and participation
- Manage balances and transactions
- Store and retrieve builds, compositions, and events
- Manage inventory and regear systems
- Enforce permissions and access controls

### 3.2 Service Improvement
We may use aggregated, anonymized data to:
- Improve Bot functionality
- Fix bugs and errors
- Analyze usage patterns
- Enhance user experience

### 3.3 Communication
We may use your information to:
- Respond to support requests
- Send important service updates (via Discord)
- Notify you of events or activities you've signed up for

## 4. Data Storage

### 4.1 Storage Location
- Your data is stored in a **MongoDB database**
- Data is organized by Discord server (guild) for isolation
- Each server's data is stored in a separate collection

### 4.2 Data Security
We implement security measures including:
- Secure database connections (MongoDB with authentication)
- Access controls and permissions
- Regular security updates
- Protection against unauthorized access

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 5.1 Server Administrators
- **Server administrators** have access to data for their specific server
- They can view, modify, and delete data within their server
- They can manage user registrations, balances, and content

### 5.2 Other Users
- Other users in your server can see:
  - Public leaderboards (attendance, balances)
  - Public builds and compositions
  - Public events and signups
  - Inventory information (if accessible)

### 5.3 Third Parties

- Sell your data to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Provide data to external services (except as required by law)

### 5.4 Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by:
- Law or legal process
- Government requests
- Protection of rights, property, or safety
